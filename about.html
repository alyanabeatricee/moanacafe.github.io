Our Story

Founded in 2015, Moana's Oasis Café was born out of a love for the ocean, the Polynesian culture, and creating memorable coffee experiences. Our mission has always been to bring people together over exceptional coffee, exotic tropical beverages, and heartfelt conversations.

Our Mission

We strive to deliver exceptional coffee experiences while supporting sustainable practices. From sourcing fair-trade beans to minimizing waste, our commitment is to both quality and the planet.

Discover More
Our Values
  • Community-Centric: Supporting local farmers and artists.
  • Sustainability: 100% compostable packaging and a waste-free initiative.
  • Innovation: Continuously experimenting with unique blends and techniques.

Our Team

Ana - Head Barista

Ana: With 10+ years of experience in crafting the perfect cup, Ana brings passion to every brew she makes. She is committed to perfecting the art of coffee and inspiring her team to deliver the best.

Expertise: Espresso techniques, Latte art, Customer relations.

Fun Fact: Ana has a secret recipe for an espresso blend that’s adored by regulars!

Alex - Barista

Alex: Alex is passionate about making every cup of coffee an experience, paying attention to every detail. He believes that the perfect cup of coffee tells a story.

Expertise: Coffee brewing, Flavor profiles, Training new baristas.

Fun Fact: Alex loves experimenting with coffee bean origins and creating new blends for the café.

Luis - Founder

Luis: Luis's vision for Moana's Oasis Café drives the concept of bringing people together over exceptional coffee. He is focused on both the quality of coffee and the community experience.

Expertise: Café management, Coffee sourcing, Sustainability initiatives.

Fun Fact: Luis has traveled to over 10 countries to meet coffee farmers and learn about sustainable sourcing practices.

Maria - Founder

Maria: Maria’s dedication to sustainability and community outreach has shaped the café into what it is today. Her work revolves around fostering local relationships and supporting sustainable practices.

Expertise: Sustainability, Community outreach, Event planning.

Fun Fact: Maria started a composting program at the café to reduce waste and promote eco-friendly habits!

Main Characters
Moana
Moana

A fearless young leader who sets out to save her people and discover the secrets of her ancestry.

Maui
Maui

The charismatic demigod who wields a magical fish hook and teams up with Moana to restore the heart of Te Fiti.

Tala
Tala

The wise and spirited grandmother who inspires Moana to embrace her destiny as a voyager.

Te Fiti
Te Fiti

The goddess who creates life and whose heart is stolen by Maui, leading to the film’s central conflict.

Tamatoa
Tamatoa

The giant, treasure-obsessed crab who resides in Lalotai, the Realm of Monsters.

Chief Tui
Chief Tui

Moana’s overprotective father and the leader of Motunui, who initially opposes her journey.

Sina
Sina

Moana’s supportive mother, who understands her daughter’s longing to explore the ocean.

Hei Hei
Hei Hei

The clueless but lovable rooster who accompanies Moana on her journey across the ocean.
